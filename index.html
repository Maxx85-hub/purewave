Swing the pure wave within.

Dear teammates of Pearl Wave, Blaze Wave, and Blizzard Wave,

This letter is written for you.

You come from three different teams.

But I'm not sure if you've noticed a small detail lately — no matter which team you're on, team names ending with "Wave" seem to be appearing more and more around the Bay Area.

This is not a coincidence.

These names were not centrally planned or assigned, but emerged naturally as we trained, grew, and spent time together, as more people stepped in and contributed their own ideas. Each team took shape on its own.

Over time, we began to realize that this was no longer just three separate teams, but a group of people meeting again and again in the same rhythm, showing up with real commitment.

Those moments of sweating together, laughing together, and pushing through together started to feel like moments worth remembering.

We call this shared space PureWave.

I'm Eva Wu, one of the initiators and co-builders of PureWave.

I grew up as an athlete, and later became a co-founder of an education technology company.

Whether in sports or in education, I've always been doing the same thing: building environments where people can stay engaged long-term, connect with one another, and grow together.

PureWave is not my idea alone. It has been shaped within the trainings, matches, and conversations you've already taken part in, gradually taking form over time.

Those seemingly small moments of joy and effort are in fact the truest expression of this community.

In PureWave, "Wave" is more than just a name. It represents rhythm and flow, and the way we move forward together through different stages.

The team you belong to may be Pearl Wave, Blaze Wave, or Blizzard Wave. No matter which Wave it is, you are already part of PureWave.

So I want to say this to you, officially: Welcome to PureWave's very first season.

This is not just a team, but a community that is actively taking shape. And you are part of what it looks like at the very beginning.

I'm really glad that this season, we get to be here together.
